Welcome to the official Asleep In The Weeds fan page!

A local Charlotte experimental jazz/funk/rock/circus group which is the creation of lead guitarist, and composer, Chad Thompson. The band developed from a Duo of Mr. Thompson and Matt Daul on Drums. Eventually it found its full form with the addition of several more very talented and seasoned musicians to create a unique orchestral combination. Their first full length album was recorded here in Charlotte just 5 years ago, and only a few have been fortunate enough to hear it!

Band Members

  • Chad Thompson - Lead Guitar
  • Matt Daul - Drums
  • Harry Kholm - Bass Guitar
  • Laurence Maher - Vocals
  • Ryan Persuad - Percussion
  • Marolyn Garo - Flute/Barritone Saxaphone
  • Lucas Wayne Ray - Backup Guitar
  • Galen Le - Keyboard
  • Marcus Harmon - Saxophone
  • David Strassburg - Violin
  • Arabo Bey - Trumpet
